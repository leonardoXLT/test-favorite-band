Maroon 5 Band Members

  • Adam Levine

    He is the lead vocalist and the main songwriter of the band. He also plays rhythm and lead guitar, occasional keyboards and bass. He is known for his distinctive voice and his tattoos. He was born on March 18, 1979 in Los Angeles, California.

  • Jesse Carmichael

    He is the keyboardist and rhythm guitarist of the band. He also provides backing vocals. He is one of the original members of the band, along with Adam Levine, Mickey Madden, and Ryan Dusick. He took a hiatus from the band during their fourth album, Overexposed (2012), but returned for their fifth album, V (2014). He was born on April 2, 1979 in Boulder, Colorado.

  • James Valentine

    He is the lead guitarist of the band. He also plays rhythm guitar, occasional keyboards, and provides backing vocals. He joined the band in 2001, after they reformed as Maroon 5 from Kara’s Flowers. He was born on October 5, 1978 in Lincoln, Nebraska.

  • Matt Flynn

    He is the drummer and percussionist of the band. He replaced Ryan Dusick in 2006, after he left the band due to injuries. He was previously a session drummer for various artists, such as Gwen Stefani, The B-52’s, and Sugar Ray. He was born on May 23, 1970 in Woodstock, New York.

  • PJ Morton

    He is the keyboardist and backing vocalist of the band. He joined the band in 2012 as a touring member, and became an official member in 2016. He is also a solo artist and a record producer, who has worked with artists such as Stevie Wonder, Lil Wayne, and Solange. He was born on March 29, 1981 in New Orleans, Louisiana.

  • Sam Farrar

    He is the bassist, keyboardist, guitarist, percussionist, and backing vocalist of the band. He joined the band in 2016 as a touring member and became an official member in 2019. He was previously a member of the band Phantom Planet, which he co-founded with Jason Schwartzman. He was born on June 29, 1978, in Los Angeles, California.

Maroon 5

Homepage Discography Bio

Maroon 5 is an American pop rock band formed in Los Angeles. They gained fame with their debut album "Songs About Jane" and have since become one of the most successful bands of all time. Known for their catchy pop hooks and energetic performances, Maroon 5 continues to dominate the charts with their blend of pop, rock, and R&B.
